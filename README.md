# Autonomous AI Payroll & Finance Platform

## 🚀 Overview
The Autonomous AI Payroll & Finance Platform is a **web‑based dual‑portal AI ecosystem** designed to serve both employers and employees through intelligent payroll, financial insights, and proactive recommendations.

Think:
**Stripe + ADP + QuickBooks + AI CFO + Personal Finance AI**  
—but autonomous, predictive, and conversational.

The system transforms payroll and finance from **manual and reactive → automated and predictive**, while maintaining strict privacy and security through a local‑computation architecture.

---

## 🎯 Core Goals
- Reduce payroll errors and compliance risks  
- Provide CFO‑level insights to employers  
- Improve financial clarity and wellbeing for employees  
- Enable predictive and proactive decision-making  
- Maintain trust through privacy‑first AI architecture  

---

# 🧩 Platform Structure

# 🔐 Privacy‑First AI Design

## Principle
AI does **not directly access raw payroll or financial records**.  
Sensitive calculations are performed locally, and only derived signals are shared with the AI.

This ensures:
- minimal data exposure  
- deterministic financial logic  
- strong auditability  
- enterprise trust  

---

# 📊 Locally Computed Metrics (AI Input Signals)

## Payroll Metrics
- Net pay verification results  
- Deduction consistency checks  
- Payroll anomaly flags + reason codes  
- Overtime spikes / irregular patterns  
- Payroll cost trends  

## Compliance Metrics
- Missing data flags  
- Filing deadline alerts  
- Threshold breach warnings  
- Risk scoring for regulatory exposure  

## Cash Flow & CFO Metrics
- Cash runway (days)  
- Burn rate (30/60/90d)  
- Payroll upcoming obligations  
- Expense trend changes  
- Overdue invoice totals + aging buckets  

## PTO & Workforce Metrics
- PTO balances  
- PTO expiration alerts  
- Workforce PTO utilization trends  
- Overtime distribution patterns  

## Benefits Metrics
- Benefits utilization rates  
- Employer match participation rates  
- Unused benefit flags  
- Enrollment anomalies  

## Employee Finance Metrics
- Estimated net pay projections  
- Budget variance signals  
- Savings progress indicators  
- Retirement contribution trends  

## Simulation Metrics
- Bonus / raise impact on net pay  
- PTO payout scenarios  
- Tax withholding impact  
- Benefits plan cost comparisons  

---

# 🧠 AI Receives Only
- Aggregated metrics  
- Risk flags and reason codes  
- Trend indicators  
- Scenario results  
- Recommended action types  

**Explicitly excluded (by default):**
- SSNs  
- Bank account numbers  
- Full payroll line items  
- Raw transaction ledgers
